Wizard Orb Escape

2024


Wizard Orb Escape is a 3D puzzle game (demo) made with Unity in a team of three.

I was one of two gameplay programmers and implemented the light refraction, UI, cutscene, and scene change mechanics (C#). I was also responsible for technical art and graphics using Unity shader graph, including the light beam shader, procedural cobblestone material, water shader, orb shader, mirror shader, and other materials. I was also the level designer, responsible for importing all art assets (3D and 2D), designing and arranging the level, hooking up scripts, and playtesting.

About


You are an evil magical spirit who has been trapped in a magical prison orb by a wizard. However, due to a mysterious cause, the prison's integrity is compromised and you escape, finding yourself in the wizard's magical tower keep! The door can only be opened by the wizard's magic. Luckily, that same magic is leaking out from the breach in your prison...

Use the mirrors, lenses, and other objects in the keep to bend, reflect, and refract the magic light beam to reach the door and open it!
