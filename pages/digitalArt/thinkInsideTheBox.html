Think Inside the Box

2023


The final project of 3D Digital Animation Techniques was a short, under 15 seconds animated film using Autodesk Maya and the Norman rig interacting with weight. I also modeled and textured the mailman's hat, package, mailbox, and ground plane (grass, sidewalk, road).

About


Mailman Norman Lily is a 26-year-old plant lover and self-proclaimed plant expert with dreams of fame and fortune from her blog titled “Plant Mother”, but her long line of dead plants says otherwise. As her latest plant dies in comedically short time, she gets a great but not thought-out idea.

Process


Treatment


I began with a proposal document in which I decided Norman would should weight interaction through pushing.

Blocking


Next was a blocking pass with the Norman rig and the objects I modeled. (The house and streetlights I downloaded from TurboSquid, though I didn't use the house in the final film.)

Spline


Then I spined the animation, added lighting, and used an HDRI downloaded from Poly Haven as a temporary background (which I also did not use in the final film).
