UF CKI Website

2024


Circle K International (CKI) is the world's largest student-led service organization and the collegiate branch of the Kiwanis service organization. As 2024-25 webmaster of the University of Florida chapter of CKI, I designed the website and created original artwork and assets for it. The 2024-25 club theme was "enchanted forest" to align with the CKI governor's literacy service project theme "storybook."
