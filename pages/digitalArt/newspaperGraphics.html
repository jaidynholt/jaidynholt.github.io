Newspaper Graphics / Infographics

2020-21


As Graphics Editor and Layout Editor of my high school newspaper, The Trinity Voice, I created vector and raster illustrations and graphics, as well as page layouts (2020 - 2022) using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

I received the first place award for informational graphics from the National Scholastic Press Association (2020) and three "Best of the Best" awards and four "All-Florida" awards for graphic design, illustration, and infographics from the Florida Scholastic Press Association (2020 - 2021).
