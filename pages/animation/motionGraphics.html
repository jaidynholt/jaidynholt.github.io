Motion Graphics

2021


I created a Death Star infomercial at the Ringling College of Art and Design Precollege Program 2021, motion design immersive, using Adobe After Effects and Illustrator.

Music credit: "Imperial starfleet deployed / city in the clouds" Star Wars Episode V: Empire Strikes Back (special edition) by John Williams

Process


The project began with thumbnail drawings and a storyboard. Then I created assets in Adobe Illustrator, imported them into After Effects, and created the animation.
