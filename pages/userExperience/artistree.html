Artistree

2024


Artistree is a fictitious slow-fashion sustainable apparel company that sources fabrics from Fab Scrap, a company who uses 100% recycled fabrics sourced from thrift stores and deadstock. The company collaborates with local artists to create designs to feature, and believes in empowering local communities to team together and create a product that will have lasting impacts to the community by combating fast fashion, producing lower carbon emissions, and uniting through art.

The project involved the creation of the brand itself, brand design identity, logos, featured apparel designs, and an interactive prototype website. I worked in a team of five as the lead prototype artist. I worked in Figma to create branded components, design the website pages, and add interactivity for a polished website prototype.
