Campus Companion

2024


Campus Companion is a navigation application specifically tailored for pedestrians on and around UF campus which will find personalized routes considering factors such as safety, commute time, accessibility, and obstructions.

I worked in a team of five as the project manager on the complete user experience journey from ideation to research and analysis to prototype and testing.
