Game Development

Giggles' Goofy Gameshow

A 3D puzzle/platformer game made with Unity where you are a contestant on a TV program from your childhood which was mysteriously taken off the air but is now back! But something doesn't feel quite right....You are forced to compete in a series of games for cash and must use your wits and skills to score high and break out of the show!

Robot Arm OpenGL Project

An interactive robot arm with a joint which moves and rotates on a XZ plane, and a projectile shoot with teleportation. This project was made with OpenGL focused on affine hierarchical transformations, drawing and lighting with shaders.

Wizard Orb Escape

A 3D puzzle game demo made with Unity in which an evil spirit must escape the wizard's keep by bending a magic light beam to open the door.

Subject N.06

A 2D puzzle adventure game made with Unity in which the lab rat N.06 must use his newfound intelligence to escape.

Minesweeper

The classic Minesweeper game coded in C++ using the SFML library.

Solar Systems Down

A 2D pixel art adventure shooter made with Unity for the 2024 FIEA Game Jam in which you play as the nomadic solar systems engineer Sol, living in a post-apocalytic, solar-punk world and fighting evil sludge.
